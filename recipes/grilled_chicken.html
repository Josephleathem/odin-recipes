chicken

to cook a chicken you need a grilled chicken breast and a skillet or a grill, depending on how you want to cook it.

The first step in cooking this is to gather all the ingredients. The second step is to melt butter in the skillet. Step number thee is to season the chicken. After the chicken has been seasoned and the butter is melted you can then add the chicken to the skillet and cook for four to five minutes before flopping and cooking it again for the same amount of time. Step by step instructions are below.

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • chicken

Steps:

  1. Gather all Items needed
  2. Melt butter in skillet or on the grill
  3. Season the chicken
  4. Cook the chicken
